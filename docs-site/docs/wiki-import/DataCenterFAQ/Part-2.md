## Frequently Asked Questions Part 2

## Hardware & Maintenance

### What is EOL (End of Life)?

The **EOL timer** indicates how long your hardware is safe. Managing your hardware lifecycle is key to keeping your data centre online:

- **Yellow Warning Mark:** When the timer runs out, the server displays this icon. This means the device is now at risk and could break at any moment.
- **Red Error Mark:** This indicates the device has officially broken and stopped working.

### How do I fix broken hardware?

You have two options for replacing EOL or broken devices:

1. **Manual Replacement:** You replace the unit yourself.
2. **Technician:** Send a technician to do it for you. The benefit of using a technician is that you do not have to re-cable the device—they handle the wiring for you.

## Support & Bug Reporting

### How do I report a bug to Waseku?

Please post in the official bug-report channel. To help Waseku find the issue faster, try to include:

- A description of what triggered the issue.
- A screenshot of the problem or your layout.

### How can I support the dev?

The best way to support **Waseku** is by being active in the community, reporting bugs clearly, and being patient as he works through the technical side of the game. Your feedback is what helps the game grow.
