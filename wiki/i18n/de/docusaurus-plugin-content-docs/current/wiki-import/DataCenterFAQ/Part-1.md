## Frequently Asked Questions Part 1

## General Information

### Who is developing the game?

The game is being developed solely by Waseku. As a solo dev, Waseku handles everything from coding and design to asset creation.

### Is there a public roadmap?

Not at this time. Waseku’s current focus is strictly on bug fixes and stability. To ensure the game is polished before adding new complexity, a formal roadmap won't be released until the current list of known issues is cleared.

## Multiplayer & Connectivity

### Is multiplayer coming to the game?

Multiplayer has been suggested and Waseku has looked into the technical requirements for it. However, it is not at the forefront of development. While Waseku may look at it again in the future, the current priority is perfecting the single-player experience.

## Modding & Customization

### Does the game support mods?

Yes! The game currently has basic mod support.

- Head over to the modding community channels to see what’s available.
- Check the pinned messages in those channels for guides provided by the community and Waseku.
