## Patch Panel

A patch panel for networking in a data center is a hardware component that organizes and manages network cable connections. It serves as a central hub where cables from various devices, like servers and switches, are connected, allowing for easier management, troubleshooting, and scalability of the network.

## Why use it?

Patch panels do not break or have EOL in the same way servers and switches do. They make life easier when you need to replace devices or rework connections.

## Practical benefit

- Cleaner cable management
- Easier device replacement
- Faster troubleshooting
- Less re-cabling when changing hardware
